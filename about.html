About Agile Pips

Agile Pips is a dynamic and innovative group of students from the University of Europe for Applied Sciences in Potsdam, Germany. We are a talented team of Digital Business and Data Science students dedicated to creating a cutting-edge weather application and website that provide accurate and reliable weather information.

Our team consists of passionate individuals with diverse skills and expertise, united by our commitment to delivering a high-quality product. As part of our academic journey, we embrace the Agile methodology, ensuring flexibility, collaboration, and continuous improvement in our development process.
